DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICS


The course of Physics is much sought-after on account of its unique curriculum design, job oriented nature, and usefulness. The course is designed to suit to the requirements of students willing to pursue masters course in Physics, Computer Applications, Information Technology, and other allied subjects. The college has huge infrastructure in terms of laboratory, Information Communication and Technology (ICT), Workshops, modern Classrooms and highly accomplished faculty members. The department organizes myriad events which provided a forum to students to engage in various activities aimed at their overall development. Keeping in view the larger interest of students, teachers of the department took various initiatives to arrange for guidance for students who wish to pursue M.Sc and other allied higher studies at Indian Institutes of Technology, IISERs etc, by qualifying the national level competitive examinations like JAM- Joint Admission Test and JEST- Joint Entrance Screening Test. The student are taken for field-trips and interaction with the experts of the field are facilitated. Teachers of the department take active interest in making our students attend useful science lectures outside the college too. Such interface of the department with the outside world of academics will go a long way in defining the changing nature of modern pedagogy. Students have also been made aware and inspired to take up internship at reputed research institutes like TIFR, NCRA, IIT etc.
